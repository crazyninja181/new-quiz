1. What is the value of the discriminant for the equation 2x² - 4x + 3 = 0?
1) 16
2) 4
3) -8
4) -4
Answer: option 4: -4
2. What is the sum of the zeroes of the quadratic polynomial x² - 7x + 10?
1) 17
2) 7
3) 10
4) 3
Answer: option 2: 7
3. If the area of a circle is 154 cm², what is its radius?
1) 5 cm
2) 6 cm
3) 7 cm
4) 8 cm
Answer: option 3: 7 cm
4. What is the distance between the points (3, 4) and (7, 1)?
1) 5 units
2) 3 units
3) 4 units
4) 6 units
Answer: option 1: 5 units
5. If the height of a cone is 12 cm and its radius is 5 cm, what is its volume?
1) 100π cm³
2) 200π cm³
3) 300π cm³
4) 400π cm³
Answer: option 3: 300π cm³
6. The coordinates of the centroid of a triangle with vertices (1, 1), (2, 3), and (3, 2) are:
1) (2, 2)
2) (1, 2)
3) (2, 1)
4) (3, 3)
Answer: option 1: (2, 2)
7. What is the probability of getting a multiple of 3 when a number from 1 to 10 is chosen at random?
1) 1/5
2) 3/10
3) 2/5
4) 4/9
Answer: option 3: 2/5
8. What is the value of sin 30° + cos 60°?
1) 1
2) 0
3) 1/2
4) 2
Answer: option 1: 1
9. What is the surface area of a sphere with radius 7 cm?
1) 154π cm²
2) 196π cm²
3) 308π cm²
4) 616π cm²
Answer: option 4: 616π cm²
10. Two tangents are drawn from an external point to a circle. What is the angle between them if they are equal in length and subtend 60° at the center?
1) 60°
2) 90°
3) 120°
4) 30°
Answer: option 2: 90°
